Case Hand washingMobile Medical Team

Each year 1.7 million children do not live to celebrate their fifth birthday because of diarrhea and pneumonia. Handwashing is among the most effective and inexpensive ways to prevent these diseases.

Problem: How can MMTI educate about how to wash your hands, and with a small budget reach a large audience of kids.

Solution: We have built a a diffusion game portal for MMTI and two Hand washing games (one for hand sanitizer and one for soap and water). Nadia, a character drawn by a student in Pakistan, is teaching us how to wash your hands.

MMTIs message is: Germs will disappear, if you wash your hands 20 seconds on both sides and between your fingers.

Result: More then 12 500 players a month and 150 000 players a year.

Check it out: us.diffusionmmti.org
Languages: English, Swahili, Spanish, Portuguese, Urdu (coming)

Countries reached: Pakistan, Mexico, Zambia, Philippines, Peru, South Africa, Colombia, Liberia, Tanzania, Bolivia, Malawi, Rwanda, Ghana, Kenya, Panama, Democratic Republic of Congo, Guatemala, Seychelles, Vanuatu

About our Client: MMTI is a nonprofit US based organization dedicated to provide medical care, disaster relief, and health education to populations in need. mobilemedicalteam.org

Case Healthy eating (coming soon to phones in many countires)Mobile Medical Team

Children who have eaten healthy food stay in school longer, attend school more regularly, learn more and become healthier. Lack of knowledge is a problem for many families.

Problem: Find a way to educate about something that is as complex as what to eat. How to tell what to eat of proteins, starchy vegetables and non starchy vegetables; And how much of each.

Solution: A game aboout healthy eating where you can choose what to have on your plate, and your choices are evaluated. The game is published on MMTI's diffusion game portal

Languages: English, Swahili, Spanish, Portuguese, Urdu, Mandarin, Arabic

Reaching countries: Pakistan, Mexico, Zambia, Philippines, Peru, South Africa, Colombia, Liberia, Tanzania, Bolivia, Malawi, Rwanda, Ghana, Kenya, Panama, Democratic Republic of Congo, Guatemala, Seychelles, Vanuatu and more...
